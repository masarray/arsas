Suggested workflow

Move from endpoint access to witnessed evidence.

Adapt the sequence to the approved FAT procedure and project responsibilities.

01

Connect and discover

Add the approved endpoint, establish MMS association and inspect the live hierarchy, DataSets, RCBs and control model.

02

Select the FAT signals

Choose status, measurement, quality and timestamp points required by the test sheet instead of mapping the entire IED blindly.

03

Validate acquisition

Prefer configured reporting, verify exact DataSet membership and keep unsupported or unavailable coverage visible.

04

Inspect GOOSE and control

Check stream identity and sequence evidence, then execute only approved Direct or SBO operations with visible completion results.

05

Confirm files and diagnostics

Probe available fault records and retain communication, report, frame and service outcomes with the originating IED.
